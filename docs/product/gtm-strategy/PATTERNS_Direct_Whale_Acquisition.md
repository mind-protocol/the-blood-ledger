## THE PROBLEM

The game does not need mass adoption; it needs a small number of high-intent whales. The GTM strategy must bypass traditional marketplaces and focus on direct acquisition and the chronicle flywheel.

---

## THE PATTERN

**Direct acquisition + player-generated distribution.** Identify high-intent communities, use Chronicles as a viral loop, and build weekly programs (Storms, Crossworld) that create shareable moments.

Current source content embedded here:
- Target player profiles
- Chronicle flywheel channels
- Weekly storm programs and Crossworld
- Direct outreach tactics

---

## PRINCIPLES

### Principle 1: 100 right players > 10,000 casuals
Focus on depth and retention, not scale.

### Principle 2: Chronicles are the ad budget
Players generate acquisition content.

### Principle 3: Programs create recurring hooks
Weekly storms and crossworld events drive sharing.
