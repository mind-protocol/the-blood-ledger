## ALGORITHM

```
1. Acquire a small cohort of high-intent players.
2. Convert sessions into Chronicles and publish them.
3. Drive inbound traffic from Chronicles to the landing experience.
4. Convert new players and loop them into Chronicle production.
5. Measure retention and adjust acquisition spend/effort.
```
