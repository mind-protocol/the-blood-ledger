# BEHAVIORS: Bleed Reports

## Observable Effects

The "Bleed Reports" are a killer feature of the Blood Ledger, designed to provide players with tangible evidence of their character's impact across the multiverse. These reports are a significant retention hook, transforming a player's character from a static entity into one with an ongoing legacy.

## Bleed Reports — Your Character's Legacy

*   **Mechanism:** Players receive regular (e.g., weekly) reports summarizing where and how their character, or their actions' consequences, have "bled into" other players' worlds.
*   **Content:** Reports detail specific instances of their character's appearance, actions, and outcomes in other Chronicles, often with excerpts or links to watch those moments.
    ```
    BLEED REPORT — Your Character's Legacy
    ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

    Your character "Aldric the Broken" has bled
    into 7 other worlds this month.

    ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

    WORLD OF @northernlion (Week 47):
    Aldric appeared as a wandering mercenary.
    He was hired to protect a caravan.
    He DIED defending the merchant's daughter
    from bandits near York.

    "He fought like a man with nothing to lose."
    — Chronicle excerpt

    [▶ WATCH THE CHRONICLE]

    ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
    ```
*   **Legacy Stats:** Reports include summary statistics on the character's overall "bleed" impact (e.g., how many worlds, how many times they died, betrayed, or saved someone).

## Why This Is Retention Gold

The Bleed Reports fundamentally alters the player's perception of their character's longevity and impact:

| Without Bleed Reports | With Bleed Reports |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| "I finished my story" | "My story is still bleeding" |
| Character is done | Character lives on |
| Why come back? | I need to see what Aldric did this week |
| Static ending | Infinite aftermath |

## The Whale Hook: Digital Immortality

This feature directly appeals to "whales" by offering a form of digital immortality. Players are not just playing a game; they are "training the souls that will haunt the multiverse," and their legacy survives their save file.

*   **Pitch:** "You're not just playing a game. You're training the souls that will haunt the multiverse. Your legacy survives your save file."
*   **Engagement:** A Monday email summarizing their character's weekly bleed reinforces this feeling of ongoing impact and encourages continued engagement.

## Steam Page Copy / Marketing Language

The Bleed Reports are central to the game's external messaging:

*   **Tagline:** "The first single-player game where your scars spread."
*   **Description:** Highlights "Your Scars Spread," "Weekly Bleed Reports," and "Digital Immortality" as key features.
