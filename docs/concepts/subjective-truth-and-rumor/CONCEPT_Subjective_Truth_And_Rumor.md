# CONCEPT: Subjective Truth & Rumor — Canon vs Belief

```
STATUS: DRAFT
CREATED: 2025-12-19
```

---

## WHAT IT IS

Subjective Truth describes the separation between **local canon (ground truth)** and **character beliefs or rumors**. Canon is immutable history; rumors and beliefs are intentionally uncertain and can be wrong.

---

## WHY IT EXISTS

The system must support conflicting beliefs without breaking the player's sense of reality. This abstraction enables misinformation, mystery, and "I was wrong" moments while keeping canon consistent.

---

## KEY PROPERTIES

- **Canon is immutable:** Ground-truth nodes and THEN chains are protected.
- **Beliefs are subjective:** Beliefs can contradict canon and each other.
- **Rumors are low-truth:** Imported or distant events default to low truth values.

---

## RELATIONSHIPS TO OTHER CONCEPTS

| Concept | Relationship |
|--------|--------------|
| Canon | Canon defines objective truth; subjective truth cannot overwrite it |
| Bleed-Through | Rumors and ghosts rely on subjective truth framing |
| Fog of War | Misinformation is a mechanic enabled by subjective truth |

---

## THE CORE INSIGHT

**Truth is local; belief is portable.** This lets the system import content safely and create drama without retconning the player's history.

---

## COMMON MISUNDERSTANDINGS

- **Not:** "Everything is subjective; nothing is real."
- **Not:** "Imported rumors become canon by default."
- **Actually:** Canon is absolute; beliefs are allowed to be wrong.
